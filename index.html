FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you write the results section for me?

No. I provide statistical analysis and interpretation guidance only. You remain responsible for writing your manuscript. However, I do provide clear interpretation notes that help you understand and describe your results.

What if I need revisions?

One round of minor revisions (clarifications, formatting adjustments) is included free. New analyses or substantial changes require a separate quote at RM100/round.

What tools do you use?

I primarily use R for statistical modelling and Python for data cleaning and exploratory analysis.

Is my data kept confidential?

Absolutely. All client data is stored securely with encryption and deleted after 12 months. I never share your data or research details without explicit permission.

Do you guarantee publication?

No one can guarantee publication. I provide rigorous, appropriate analysis that meets academic standards, but publication depends on many factors, including study design, reviewers, and journal policies.

What file formats do you accept?

CSV, Excel (.xlsx, .xls), SPSS (.sav), Stata (.dta), and most common data formats. If you have an unusual format, just ask!
